# FanCash Trading Card Redemption V1

Source: FanCash_Trading_Card_Redemption_V1.docx

## 1. Overview

The FanCash Trading Card is a gamified collectible insert program that bridges collecting and fantasy sports. Collectors pull FanCash Trading Card inserts from standard packs (starting with 2026 Topps Flagship Football), scratch off a code, and redeem it on Fanatics Collect. Once redeemed, the actual player card ships to their vault or home, and the collector begins earning FanCash based on that player's real-world performance throughout the season.

Cards immediately appear in the user's vault and collection upon redemption. Users earn FanCash weekly by holding the card. FanCash earnings are tied to the redeemer's FanID and are non-transferable — even if the physical card is sold on the marketplace, the original redeemer continues earning.

### Why this matters

FanCash Trading Cards create a unique value proposition that only Collect can deliver. Unlike pulling a card and putting it in a binder, Collect lets users showcase FanCash cards in public collections, track live athlete performance, earn real FanCash rewards, and sell their redeemed cards.

### Programme scope

Pilot with Topps Flagship Football in August 2026, fast follow to NBA (October 2026) and MLB Series 1 (February 2027). Initial scope: 25 NFL players (15 QB, 5 RB, 5 WR) across 4 tiers, totaling 6,525 cards.

## 4. Programme mechanics

### 4.1 Tier structure & card volume

- **Silver** — un-numbered, high-volume, 1x multiplier (scalable entry-level tier)
- **Gold** — 2x multiplier
- **Black** — 10x multiplier
- **Platinum** — 20x multiplier

### 4.2 FanCash earn mechanics (Football)

Modeled against Fantasy Football stats. Earnings calculated per stat and multiplied by card tier. QB cards intentionally earn more than WR/RB. Negative points for INTs and fumbles are flat across all tiers.

### 4.3 Payout examples (based on 2024 stats)

- Minimum Silver tier: ~$100 per card per season
- Maximum Platinum: exceeds $8,000 for top performers

### 4.4 Redemption & earning rules

- Redemption window: 10 years from card distribution
- Earnings begin at season start; retroactive payout upon redemption
- Unredeemed inserts can be traded, gifted, or sold before scratching
- Once redeemed, FanCash linkage is non-transferable (tied to FanID permanently)
- Physical card can be sold on marketplace; original redeemer continues earning
- Weekly payouts on Tuesdays based on prior week performance
- Earnings ONLY for the player's designated season
- End-of-season awards (MVP, etc.) provide bonus payouts

## 5. User stories

### As a collector...

- Pull a FanCash insert and redeem on Collect to start earning and see card in vault immediately
- Simple redemption process for new-to-Collect users
- Instant gratification: card appears in vault/collection immediately upon redemption
- Live stat tracking with FanCash earnings translation
- Tuesday payout notifications for excitement and engagement
- Portfolio view: all FanCash cards, tiers, and cumulative earnings in one place
- Tier/player comparison to understand relative value
- Standard marketplace experience for selling physical cards
- Community: see which collectors own cards for the same player
- Social sharing: share progress, big weeks, and milestones
- Leaderboard: highest-earning cards and collectors for motivation
- Dedicated landing page explaining the program and value proposition
- Results checker: look up any player's performance (not just owned cards)

### As Vault Operations...

- Handle immediate vault ingestion for redeemed cards
- Understand whether unredeemed inserts will be accepted in vault

### As Finance...

- Visibility into total FanCash payout exposure per week and per season
- Distribution of payouts across tiers and positions for risk validation

### As Customer Support...

- Handle failed or already-redeemed scratch-off codes
- Explain non-transferable FanCash rules
- Answer questions about low/zero payouts (injury, bye week, poor performance)

## 7. Feature areas (V1)

### FanCash Trading Card Hub
Central landing page within the Redemption Hub. Entry point for education, code entry, player results checker, program leaderboard, and season calendar.

### Athlete progress in collection / portfolio
Dynamic card data: tier, player, season stats, cumulative FanCash, weekly breakdown, live stat integration, earnings projection, payout history, award tracker. Prominently displayable in public collections for community engagement and FOMO.

### Community & FOMO features
Social layer: offer-driven engagement from public collections, activity feed with trending transactions and big earners, collector leaderboards (total earned, most valuable portfolio, most cards held).

### Social media sharing
Shareable experiences for cards and progress. Format TBD by design team — goal is to make weekly earnings, big moments, and milestones compelling to share.

### Marketplace
FanCash cards follow existing marketplace policy and UX. No special treatment needed. FanCash earnings remain tied to original redeemer's FanID.
