# FanCash Trading Card — Football proposal (December 2025)

Source: 260129_FanCash Trading Card FB Proposal_vCurrent.pptx (12 slides)

## Collector achievement programmes overview

FanCash Card is one of several collector achievement programs ("micro-wins" for collectors), alongside upcoming programs like HOF Superfractor, Jigsaw, and Ginter Games.

## Executive summary

Collectible "rewards" card allowing customers to earn FanCash based on player performance each week.

- Pilot with Topps Flagship FB in August 2026, fast follow with NBA (October 2026) and MLB Series 1 (February 2027)
- Initial scope: 25 players (15 QB, 5 RB, 5 WR) across 4 tiers — 6,525 total cards
- FanCash payout loosely modeled against Fantasy Football stats
- Card redemption window of 10 years; earnings begin only after redemption, but unredeemed cards retain value
- Redemption process to live on Fanatics Collect

## Card tiers and mechanics

- **Silver** — 200 per subject, un-numbered, earns 1x points per stat (entry-level growth lever)
- **Gold** — 50 per subject, earns 2x points per stat
- **Black** — 10 per subject, earns 10x points per stat
- **Platinum** — 5 per subject, earns 20x points per stat

Stats tracked are similar to Fantasy Football. FanCash payouts occur weekly on Tuesdays.

### Updated proposal (Silver tier addition)

- Expands reach and excitement without diluting FanCash earn mechanics
- Creates a scalable, un-numbered tier for future growth
- 6,525 total FanCash cards across 4 tiers (+300% from original)
- Updated risk: ~$2.0–$2.5M in FanCash for first FB release
- 42% of total payout in Silver, 25% Gold, 27% Black, 6% Platinum
- QB cards intentionally earn more than WR/RB, following Fantasy FB formatting

## Payout examples (based on 2024 stats)

- Minimum Silver tier earning targeted at ~$100 per card
- Maximum Platinum payout potential exceeds $8,000 for top performers
- Gold card max earning ~$1.6K; highest-value Platinum cards worth $8K+
- Illustrative players: Joe Burrow, Lamar Jackson, Jalen Hurts, Saquon Barkley, Derrick Henry, Jonathan Taylor, Ja'Marr Chase, Justin Jefferson, CeeDee Lamb

## Estimated risk

- Assuming each player plays 18 games and is healthy, estimated max risk: ~$3.4M
- Drops to ~$2.7M with injury considerations, playoff scenarios, and 90% redemption rate

## Collector journey (two options)

### Option 1: Pull card and redeem
1. Customer buys 2026 Flagship box and pulls a FanCash redemption card
2. Customer scratches off code and redeems on Collect.com
3. Customer receives player card in mail or in Vault
4. FanCash linked to FanID — earns weekly based on player performance

### Option 2: Pull card and sell redemption
1. Customer A pulls redemption card but sells the unredeemed insert to Customer B
2. Customer B scratches off code and redeems on Collect.com
3. Customer B gets FanCash linked to their FanID and physical card
4. Original seller earns nothing (FanCash goes to redeemer)

- Could pull card mid-season or at any point in 10-year window
- FanCash earn is ONLY for the player's designated season and tier

## Roadmap

Pilot: Topps Flagship Football (August 2026) → NBA (October 2026) → MLB Series 1 (February 2027)
