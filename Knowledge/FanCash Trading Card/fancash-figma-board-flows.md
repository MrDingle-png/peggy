## User stories by area

### Acquisition & redemption

- **As a** collector who pulls a FanCash Trading Card from a pack, **I want** a clear way to redeem my code within the FanCash Trading Card Redemption flow, **so that** I can stake my claim for the potential/earned FanCash and then decide what to do with the resulting card.
- **As a** new collector discovering Collect through FanCash Trading Card Redemption, **I want** a guided account creation and redemption flow, **so that** I can successfully create an account and complete redemption without abandoning the process.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** immediate confirmation that my redemption succeeded and that my card appears in my Collection, **so that** I trust the system and feel confident my earnings tracking has begun.
- **As a** collector who owns a FanCash Trading Card but cannot redeem due to a damaged, unreadable, or rejected code, **I want** a clearly visible remediation path within the redemption flow, **so that** I understand how to resolve the issue and do not feel permanently excluded from the program.

### Ongoing engagement

- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** to see how my player's real-world performance translates into FanCash earnings, **so that** I understand how payouts are calculated and feel connected to weekly performance.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** to be notified when my weekly FanCash payout is calculated and applied, **so that** I return to Collect and stay engaged with the program.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer with one or more redeemed cards, **I want** a unified portfolio view within my Collection that displays each card's tier, cumulative earnings, and current status, **so that** I can understand my total FanCash performance in one place.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** contextual comparison across tiers and players, **so that** I understand the relative earning potential and positioning of my redeemed card.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** clear visibility into when earning eligibility begins and ends, **so that** I understand the time horizon of my earning potential.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** access to a detailed historical breakdown of how each weekly payout was calculated, **so that** I can verify totals and understand how player performance translated into earnings.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** clear documentation explaining how official stat corrections impact payouts, **so that** I understand how potential adjustments may occur.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** to be notified if a previously issued payout is adjusted due to stat corrections, **so that** changes do not feel arbitrary or erroneous.

### Social & community layer

- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** visibility into other redeemers who hold FanCash Trading Cards tied to the same player, **so that** I feel part of a shared competitive ecosystem.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** a ranked view of highest-earning FanCash Trading Cards and redeemers, **so that** I can benchmark my performance and feel motivated to improve my standing.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** to share my redeemed card's performance milestones externally, **so that** I can showcase strong earning weeks and increase awareness of the program.

### Marketplace & liquidity

- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer who owns a vaulted card, **I want** to list my card using the standard marketplace flow, **so that** selling follows the same experience as other eligible collectibles.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer listing my redeemed card for sale, **I want** clear confirmation that I will retain all FanCash earning rights after sale, **so that** I understand the consequences of selling the physical card.
- **As a** marketplace buyer, **I want** clear visibility into whether a listed FanCash Trading Card is unredeemed or already redeemed, **so that** I can intentionally purchase based on earning eligibility.
- **As a** prospective buyer of a redeemed FanCash Trading Card, **I want** clear disclosure that FanCash earnings remain permanently tied to the original redeemer, **so that** I understand that purchasing the card does not transfer earning rights.

### Discovery & consideration

- **As a** prospective buyer, **I want** a dedicated landing page explaining how FanCash Trading Card Redemption works, how earnings are calculated, and potential payout scenarios, **so that** I can evaluate whether purchasing a pack or secondary card is worthwhile.
- **As a** prospective buyer, **I want** to view performance and historical earnings for all players participating in the program, **so that** I can make informed purchase decisions.
- **As a** prospective buyer purchasing on a secondary marketplace, **I want** clear educational guidance on how to identify an unredeemed FanCash Trading Card (e.g., intact scratch panel), **so that** I understand how earning eligibility is determined.
- **As a** FanCash Trading Card Redeemer, **I want** visibility into how FanCash earnings are treated for tax and reporting purposes, **so that** I understand the financial implications.

### Vault operations

- **As a** Vault Operations team member, **I want** successful redemptions to automatically transfer ownership of already vaulted cards into the collector's Collection, **so that** collectors experience immediate ownership.
- **As a** Vault Operations team member, **I want** clarity on whether unredeemed FanCash Trading Cards are eligible for vault intake, **so that** we can forecast operational capacity and define handling policies.
- **As a** Vault Operations team member, **I want** a defined intake and authentication process for unredeemed cards, **so that** we can verify scratch panel integrity, confirm authenticity, and ensure the code has not already been redeemed.
- **As a** Vault Operations team member, **I want** a defined process to validate and redeem vaulted, unredeemed cards on behalf of the owning collector, **so that** ownership can be transferred accurately and securely.
- **As a** collector who owns a vaulted, unredeemed FanCash Trading Card, **I want** the ability to request that Vault Operations redeem the card on my behalf, **so that** I can receive the associated redeemed card into my Collection without withdrawing the physical redemption card.
- **As a** collector submitting a FanCash Trading Card to the Vault, **I want** the submission flow to allow me to clearly identify the item as an unredeemed FanCash Trading Card, **so that** it is processed under the correct intake and redemption workflow.

### Finance & risk

- **As a** Finance team member, **I want** reporting on projected and actual weekly and seasonal payout exposure, **so that** we can validate financial risk against approved models.
- **As a** Finance team member, **I want** visibility into payout distribution across tiers, players, and positions, **so that** we ensure program economics remain within approved thresholds.
- **As a** Risk or Compliance team member, **I want** monitoring of suspicious redemption activity (e.g., bulk redemptions, exploit attempts, anomalous patterns), **so that** we protect program integrity and financial exposure.

### Customer support

- **As a** Customer Support team member, **I want** tooling to validate redemption code status and history, **so that** I can resolve invalid or duplicate code cases efficiently.
- **As a** Customer Support team member, **I want** clear documentation that FanCash earnings are tied to the original redeemer's FanID and do not transfer, **so that** I can set accurate expectations and prevent disputes.
- **As a** Customer Support team member, **I want** visibility into weekly performance inputs used to calculate payouts, **so that** I can explain low or zero payouts accurately.
